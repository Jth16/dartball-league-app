# Labelle Dartball League — App Overview

**Live site:** [ltvfdartball.com](https://ltvfdartball.com)

The Labelle Dartball League app is the official web application for tracking standings, player statistics, game results, and schedules for the Labelle Dartball League. It is updated throughout the season and serves as the central hub for players, team captains, and fans to follow the league.

---

## What Is Dartball?

Dartball is a dart-throwing game played on a diamond-shaped board that mirrors a baseball field. Players throw darts at the board to simulate at-bats — hitting singles, doubles, triples, home runs, or making outs. Teams play through innings, and the team with the most runs at the end of the game wins. It combines the strategy of baseball with the skill of darts.

---

## Features

### Standings
Displays the current league standings for all teams, including:
- Wins, Losses, Win Percentage, and Games Behind the leader
- Standings are computed in real time from all recorded game results
- Clinching indicators shown when a team has mathematically secured 1st or 2nd place

### All Stats
A tabbed section containing multiple views:

- **Leaders** — Individual player leaderboards for Hits, Singles, Doubles, Triples, Home Runs, and Batting Average (minimum games played required for average title)
- **Players** — Full player stats for every team, including Singles, Doubles, Triples, Home Runs, Batting Average, Games Played, and At-Bats
- **Games** — Game-by-game result leaders and scoring highlights
- **Results** — Full game results organized by date and matchup
- **Playoffs** — Playoff bracket and results
- **H2H (Head-to-Head)** — Matrix showing every team's win-loss record against every other team for the season

### Schedule
The full season schedule listing all matchups and dates.

### Rules
The official dartball rules for the league.

## Season Format

- The regular season consists of **96 games per team**
- **9 teams** compete in the league
- Regular season standings determine seeding for the playoffs
- **Playoffs** are a multi-round bracket:
  - An 8 vs. 9 play-in game (Best of 3)
  - Round 1 — four series (Best of 5)
  - Round 2 — two series (Best of 5)
  - Championship Game (Best of 7) with a 3rd place consolation series (Best of 5)
